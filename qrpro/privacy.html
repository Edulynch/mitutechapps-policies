Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how QR Pro – Scanner & Generator ("QR Pro", "the App") handles data and protects your privacy. QR Pro is developed and published by MituTech Apps.

1. App Controller

MituTech Apps
Official website: https://mitutechapps.com
Contact email: eduardolynch.consultas@gmail.com

2. Data We Do Not Collect

QR Pro is designed with a privacy-first approach. We do not directly collect, store, or sell personal information.

The App does not directly collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers.
  • User accounts or credentials.
  • Precise location data (GPS).
  • Contacts, calendars, or address books.
  • Photos or videos from your gallery (except those you explicitly select).
  • Device identifiers collected directly by us (such as IMEI).

Advertising identifiers and technical data may be processed by third-party services (see Section 5).

3. Data Stored Locally on Your Device

QR Pro may store certain information only on your device, such as scan history or generated QR codes. This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly share it.

  • Scanned QR content (URLs, text, Wi-Fi data, etc.).
  • Generated QR code data.
  • Timestamps and optional user notes.

When you uninstall the App, all locally stored data is permanently deleted.

4. Device Permissions

  • Camera: Required exclusively for real-time QR scanning. All image processing happens locally on your device. No images or video streams are transmitted or stored remotely.
  • Photos / Storage: Used only when you choose to save or scan a QR image. Access is limited to system-provided pickers and sharing mechanisms.

5. Third-Party Services

QR Pro integrates third-party SDKs to fund development and ensure app stability.

Google AdMob (Advertising)

AdMob may process:

  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID / IDFA).
  • Ad impressions and interactions.
  • Approximate location (country/region).
  • Device type, OS version, and anonymized IP address.

You can opt out of personalized ads via your device settings.

Firebase (Analytics & Remote Config)

  • App usage statistics and performance metrics.
  • Crash reports and diagnostics.
  • Basic device information (model, OS version).
  • Feature flag configuration.

Firebase Remote Config is not used to track individual users or to create user profiles.

Privacy policies:

  • Google & AdMob Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Privacy & Security

6. Safe Browsing (Optional)

When scanning QR codes containing URLs, QR Pro may check the link against Google Safe Browsing to warn about potentially harmful sites. Only the URL text is processed for this purpose.

7. Advertising, Consent & Your Choices

QR Pro displays ads to keep the app free.

  • EEA / GDPR: You may be asked for consent regarding personalized advertising.
  • CCPA / Other regions: You may limit ad personalization via your device settings.

8. Children’s Privacy

QR Pro is not designed for children and is not part of the Google Play Designed for Families program. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Data Security

Most processing occurs locally on your device. Data transmitted to third-party services is protected using industry-standard encryption (HTTPS/TLS).

10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, contact:
eduardolynch.consultas@gmail.com
