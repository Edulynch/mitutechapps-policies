Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 19, 2025

Thank you for using Sudoku Challenge. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle data and protect your privacy.

1. App Controller

Sudoku Challenge – MituTech Apps
Contact email: eduardolynch.consultas@gmail.com

2. Personal and Identifiable Information

Sudoku Challenge is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or share any Personally Identifiable Information (PII).

The app does NOT request or manage:

  • Name, email address, or phone number.
  • Contact lists or address book.
  • Photos, media files, or documents.
  • Precise location (GPS).

3. Device Permissions

Sudoku Challenge does not require special permissions to function. Game progress and preferences are stored locally on your device.

4. Third-Party Data Collection (SDKs)

Although the app does not collect personal data directly, it uses third-party services for advertising, analytics, and in-app purchases. These services may collect technical data and device identifiers.

Services used:

  • Google AdMob: To display advertisements.
  • Firebase (Analytics & Remote Config): To analyze app usage, crashes, and performance.
  • RevenueCat: To manage in-app purchases and subscriptions.

Data these services may collect:

  • Advertising identifiers (Google Advertising ID).
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks).
  • Technical device information (model, OS version, language, anonymized IP).
  • Purchase and subscription status.
  • Diagnostic data (crash logs).

Payments are processed securely by Google Play. We do not have access to your payment information.

Links to provider privacy policies:

  • Google & AdMob Policy
  • Firebase Privacy
  • RevenueCat Privacy

5. Advertising and Consent (GDPR / CCPA)

  • Users in Europe (GDPR): You may be asked to consent to personalized or non-personalized ads.
  • Rest of the World: Ads are served using advertising identifiers. You can limit ad tracking in your device settings.

6. Children

Sudoku Challenge is not specifically directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Security

We use standard security practices and secure connections (HTTPS) when communicating with third-party services.

8. Your Rights and Contact

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at:
eduardolynch.consultas@gmail.com
